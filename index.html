For Those That Have No Time

What is Medici?

Medici automatically moves money between your accounts so that you don’t need to remember to pay your bills or add to your savings. We got you covered.

For Those That Plan Ahead

How Medici Works

We get it, you're busy; why stress about money? Medici uses A.I. to make sure that there is always enough in your account when you need it, and plans out your savings & investments so that you can be confident in your financial future.

Medici SpearMint

Details of our algorithms

We developed a host of inhouse algorithms that rely on statistical inferencing, folding algorithms and classical deep learning techniques to form a hybrid model trained on your financial data. We like to call it Medici Spearmint. But you can just call it magic.

For those that like to be savvy

Life gets in the way, and you get distracted from what's important. Medici will pay down your credit cards smartly to optimize your credit score & keep your account running smoothly.

No Hidden Fees.

Fees

But for less than a week's worth of coffee, Medici Prenium will give you access to intelligent budgeting, improved interest and a host of features your old bank never thought of.

But Safety's First

Security

Medici algorithm goes through multiple checks to make sure your money is where it needs to be.

We take security very seriously, so all effort has been made to pass the bar on the industry standards of data safety.
