What is Medici?

Having a budget, and a bank is a lot of work. We are your bank and your budget, so there is no need to juggle all of those apps and that old excel spreadsheet.

A Budget That Learns

How Medici Works

We get it, you're busy; why stress about money? Medici's budgeting tool learns how you spend and helps to keep you on track using your daily financial data.

Make Goals and Meet Them

Auto Budgeting

Life gets in the way, and you get distracted from what's important. Medici will help you meet your targets by changing your budget to save for what you need.

No Hidden Fees.

Fees

You pay more for a week's worth of coffee than you do for Medici. We charge you a small monthly amount so we don't have to charge you transfer fees, minimum account balances, overdraft fees, or any of that nonsense like everyone else does!

Safety First

Security

Safety First - connecting your bank to a bunch of apps isn't the smarted thing to do with your money. Medici puts your finances in one safe place so that we can keep a watchful eye with end-to-end encryption and security that you control.

We take security very seriously, so all effort has been made to pass the bar on the industry standards of data safety.
