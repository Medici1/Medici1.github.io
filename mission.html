Our Mission

“Medici exists to free people from financial complexity.”

Breaking down the Mission

Financial complexity

The financial word is very complicated, for a few reasons.

Firstly, money is complicated. To be good with it, you need to understand incomings & outgoings, investments, the economy … and that’s just the financial bit.

Then there are all the mechanisms, technologies, and terms used by the financial world that even industry veterans don’t fully understand.

But all-in-all, it’s like this for a reason. The banking world learned (american’s prefer learned) a long time ago that people don’t actually pay that much attention to the numbers. So, they decided to keep all of these mechanisms, terms, and fees as opaque as they could because it’s good for their business.

We are not saying that “banks are evil.” However, they are just in the business of making money, and keeping it complicated helps them do just that.

Medici’s mission is to help you navigate this complex world. We have spent our lives understanding the financial landscape, technology, & terms and are simplifying where we can so that you don’t need to worry about it.

We hope to see a world where financial management can exist in harmony with the customer - with you.

We are working towards a society where banks don’t need to exist by feeding off of you, confusing you, and taking advantage of you.

That is the world Medici wants to build.

To free people

Financial management is a lot of work. A LOT.

To do it well, you need to have an army of apps, keep spreadsheets, maintain tight budgets, and constantly watch the markets.

However, with the fast progress of technology today, Medici believes that we can lift some of this burden for you.

We take AI & machine learning and apply them to your financial world, so that Medici can lift away the burden of daily financial management.

In the future, we envision a world where your money is cared for, managed, and grown with highly-intelligent algorithms that truly understand your needs.

Our banking app is just the beginning … who knows where we can go from here.
